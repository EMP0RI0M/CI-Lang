The defining moment for Chaos Intelligence was the **Sine Wave Extraction Experiment**. This experiment proved that meaningful, deterministic signals can be harvested from a purely chaotic swarm without direct control of the agents.

- **Chaos Strength**: Global entropy $E = 0.6$, driving agents into the "boiling" regime.
- **Goal**: Reconstruct a $10Hz$ sine wave from the swarm's emergent fluctuations.

#### 5.2. Execution
Instead of training the agents, we trained a **FluxTorch Readout Layer** to "listen" to the swarm. Using **Entropy-Driven Gradient Descent (EGD)**, the readout identified agents whose chaotic trajectories were weakly correlated with the target signal through the coupling matrix.

#### 5.3. Result
The system achieved a **Mean Squared Error (MSE) of 0.002**. 
- The swarm provided the "reservoir" of infinite trajectories.
- The readout provided the "selective collapse" into meaning.
- Computation occurred with **zero backpropagation** through the agents themselves, saving massive computational energy.
